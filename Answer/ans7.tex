\Ans{$f(x)=3x^2-12x+2$
	
	At $x=0$
	$$f(0)=2$$
	$$f(1)=-7$$
	So, Roots between $0$ and $1$ is $[0,1]$
	$$f'(x)=6x-12$$
	Now, by using newton-raphson method
	$$x_{n+1}=x_n-\frac{f(x)}{f'(x)}$$
	take $x_0=0.5$
	$$x_{n+1}=x_n-\frac{3{x_n}^2-12x_n+2}{6x_n-12}$$
	
	$1^{st}$ iteration:
	$$x_1=0.5-\frac{3(0.5)^2-12(0.5)+2}{6(0.5)-12}$$
	
	$$x_1=0.13889$$
	
	$2^{nd}$ iteration:
    $$x_2=0.13889-\frac{3{x_1}^2-12x+2}{6(0.13889)-12}$$
    $$x_2=0.17392$$
    $3^{rd}$ iteration:
    $$x_3=0.17392-\frac{3(0.17392)^2-12(0.17392)+2}{6(0.17392)-12}$$
    $$x_3=0.174256$$
    
    $4^{th}$ iteration:
    
    $$x_4=0.174256-\frac{3(0.174256)^2-12(0.174256)+2}{6(0.174256)-12}$$
    $$x_4=0.174257$$
    
    So, real root of equation is $0.174257$}
